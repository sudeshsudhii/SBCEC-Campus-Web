Welcome to Sri Balaji Chockalingam Engineering College

Thirumathi Kannamal Educational Trust, was founded in the year 1985 with the main objectives to foster Higher Education in TamilNadu. Under this trust, various institutions, under the umbrella known as A.C.S.Group of Institutions, were established by our Founder-Chairman Thiru.A.C.Shanmugam, former M.P., and M.L.A., as manifestation of his pioneering vision and forethought. Sri Balaji Chokalingam Engineering College is one among them.

Sri Balaji Chockalingam Engineering College, an institution of academic excellence, was established in the year 1999, with a cherished desire to serve the cause of humanity through technical higher education. This college has been affiliated to Anna University, Chennai and has been recognized by the Government of TamilNadu. It is a Co-Educational Engineering College offering six B.E., / B.Tech., courses and four P.G. courses. All the courses have been recognized by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi and accredited by NBA. Read More
